What Needs to Improve

Recommendations to increase AI visibility

Based on the data across all three markets, these are the highest-impact areas to address.

Critical · All Markets

Claude visibility is zero across all seven locations

AllSouth does not appear in any Claude responses in any market. Claude is one of the fastest-growing AI assistants. The fix: structured content with clear entity markup, FAQ schema, and answer-shaped formatting that Claude's retrieval system can parse and cite.

Critical · Expansion Markets

Nashville scores 12%, Panama City 23%, and Fort Walton Beach 37%

Nashville, Panama City, and Fort Walton Beach significantly trail the core Alabama markets. Nashville appears in AI results just 12% of the time, with zero visibility in the grill retailer category across all platforms. These newer markets need dedicated local content, citation builds, and Google Business Profile optimization. These locations need dedicated local content, citation builds, and Google Business Profile optimization urgently.

Critical · Multiple Markets

Google AI Mode returns near-zero results in two markets

AI Mode (Google's AI-powered search) shows AllSouth in 0% of Montgomery prompts, only 7% in Panama City, and 20% in Nashville. This is Google's next-generation search experience. The fix: strengthen local SEO signals, Google Business Profile completeness, and structured data for these locations specifically.

High Priority · All Markets

Grill Retailer category is the weakest across every market

Birmingham 35%, Huntsville 40%, Montgomery 26%, Fort Walton Beach 10%, Panama City 5%, and Chattanooga and Nashville both at 0%. AI engines do not associate AllSouth strongly with grills and outdoor cooking. The fix: dedicated grill content pages, outdoor living category pages, brand-specific grill pages (Traeger, Big Green Egg, Kamado Joe, etc.), and citation listings in outdoor/grill directories.

High Priority · Expansion Markets

National chains dominate AllSouth outside Alabama

In Fort Walton Beach, Panama City, Chattanooga, and Nashville, national chains like Lowe's, Home Depot, and Best Buy consistently out-mention AllSouth. The independent competitor landscape is thinner here, meaning there is an opportunity to become the go-to local alternative if visibility improves. Local content, reviews, and community-focused citations would differentiate AllSouth from the big box stores.

High Priority · All Markets

Competitor Cohen's is out-citing AllSouth in Montgomery

thinkcohens.com has 69 citations versus myallsouth.com's 50 in the Montgomery market. When AI engines see a competitor cited more frequently, they tend to recommend that competitor more often. The fix: increase AllSouth's citation footprint through directory submissions, press mentions, and content syndication.

Opportunity · Core Markets

Gemini and Grok are strong foundations to build on

Gemini averages 82% and Grok averages 82% across the Alabama markets. These are AllSouth's strongest platforms. The opportunity: study what makes AllSouth visible on these platforms (likely strong review profiles and structured business data) and replicate those signals for the weaker platforms and Florida locations.

Opportunity · Birmingham

Birmingham is the strongest market at 56%, use it as the model

Whatever content, citations, and local SEO work is driving Birmingham's visibility should be replicated for all other locations. Birmingham's visibility is nearly five times Nashville's.

Opportunity · All Markets

Over half of all prompts are missed entirely

Across all seven markets, the majority of the time a customer asks AI a relevant question, AllSouth is not mentioned at all. Each missed prompt is a potential customer asking for help and not hearing AllSouth's name. The fix: expand content to cover more query types, especially long-tail questions about delivery, installation, financing, and brand-specific appliance comparisons.
